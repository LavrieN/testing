Mechanical parts.

List of mechanical parts.

Gear:
Decription: A circular pieces with "teeth" that can grind with another gear to tranfer rotation. Gear of different ration can be connect with each other to increase / decrease speed with side effect of increasing or decreasing toqure.

Chain drive: Decription: Chain drive is multiple particuilar pieces of metal linked together to form a chain, the link is designed to be fitted on gears to tranfer rotation over longer distance.

Belt drive: Decription: Belt drive is similar to chain drive but the chain is now made out of a flexible material which allow it to be used in different application. Belt can be driven by teeth (V-belt drive) or friction(flat belt drive), friction driven can help add a speed limit to the set.
